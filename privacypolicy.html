No More Privacy Policy

No More doesn't collect, track, or store any personally identifiable information. When you open the No More app, it sends a few things to a private server I made and control:

  • A random identifier unique to your installation of the app
  • The version of the app, iOS operating system, and of the No More settings you're using
  • What, if anything, has been purchased
  • What rules are currently enabled

No More doesn't share that data with anyone. We do log and store data in order to help us debug the app, though only I am authorized access to those systems. No More does not connect your information to advertising or other tracking systems.

Also, I pledge to delete any and all user-related data if No More or Strings.fm Inc. ever changes control to someone other than me, Andrew Konoff.

In effect as of June 4, 2020.
